Coaching at Cosmic League is bespoke — pricing is agreed directly with each client, based on the scope, nature, and duration of the engagement. All pricing discussions are held in complete confidence, and there is no pressure to commit during any early conversation.

Policy

Refund Policy

Cosmic League operates on a formal contractual basis. Once a coaching agreement has been signed and payment has been made, no refunds will be issued under any circumstances.

This policy reflects the nature of the engagement: coaching at Cosmic League is a long-term, subscription-based relationship. The commitment of time, preparation, and resources begins at the point of contract — not at a later session.

Clients cannot purchase coaching services directly through this website. Every engagement is initiated through a personal application, a direct conversation, and a formal agreement. This process is designed to ensure that when an engagement begins, both parties have full confidence it is the right fit. We do not expect — and have not encountered — refund requests, because we do not begin work until that mutual confidence exists.

If you have any questions about this policy before entering an engagement, please raise them during your discovery conversation.
