Policy

Refund Policy

Once a coaching contract is signed and paid, no refunds are issued.

Coaching with us is a long term, subscription based engagement. The work begins at the point of contract, not at any later session.

Clients cannot buy coaching directly on this site. Every engagement starts with an application, a private conversation, and a signed agreement. By the time work begins, both sides are confident in the fit. We do not get refund requests because we do not start work until that confidence is established.

Any questions about this policy should be raised during the first conversation.
